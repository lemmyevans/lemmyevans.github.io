River Bend Farm Bed & Breakfast Guest Rooms

River Bend Farm Bed & Breakfast is the ideal venue for gatherings of small families and groups of friends. We encourage groups to rent out all bedrooms and enjoy the entire house including the keeping room and tap room.
In response to Covid-19 Restrictions we now offer two guest bedrooms each with private bathrooms or all rooms with two shared bathrooms.
Our rooms start at $150 per night plus state and local tax. All bedrooms have air conditioning and Wi-Fi. Rates include a served home-made continental breakfast each morning.
We do request a two-night minimum stay on summer weekends and a three-night say minimum on holidays and selected Williams College weekends.
Please note that we only accept Cash and Checks. We look forward to your visit.
